Local Expertise

Duluth Hair Care Guide

Living near Lake Superior means dealing with unique environmental factors. Here is Meagan's professional advice for keeping your blonde bright and hair healthy in Northern Minnesota.

Managing Duluth's Water

Duluth water can contain mineral deposits that make blonde hair appear brassy or dull over time. I highly recommend using a clarifying chelating shampoo once every two weeks followed by a deep hydration mask to preserve tone.

Winter Protection

North Shore winters are famously harsh. Cold air outside and dry heat inside steal moisture, leaving colored hair brittle. Incorporate a leave-in conditioner and a weekly moisture treatment to maintain cuticle elasticity.

UV Defense

Summer sun reflecting off Lake Superior accelerates blonde oxidation and fading. When spending time outdoors in Canal Park or hiking the SHT, apply a hair mist with UV protection to keep your color looking fresh.
